questions

Frequently asked.

Do I need prior coding experience?
Not at all. Phase 1 starts from absolute zero. If you've never written a line of code, that's exactly where we begin. The AI tools actually make this easier than ever for beginners.
Why learn to code if AI can code for me?
Because AI code breaks. It hallucinates. It makes architectural decisions you'll regret. The people who will thrive aren't those who avoid coding — they're the ones who understand it well enough to direct, fix, and improve what AI generates. That's exactly what we teach.
What countries do you accept students from?
Anywhere with a stable internet connection. We specifically work with international students because it lets us offer competitive pricing while building a genuinely diverse cohort. We're timezone-flexible and accommodate evenings and weekends.
What currency do you charge in?
We accept USD, EUR, and GBP depending on your location. Payment is handled securely online per session block. We'll confirm pricing on your free trial call.
What age group is StackUp for?
Primarily high school students (Grades 8–12) and first-year university students. Phase 1 is accessible from around age 13 and up. The curriculum scales with the student.
Can I do both the Tech and Maths tracks?
Yes — many students do, and they complement each other well. We'll make sure sessions don't clash and that the workload is realistic for your school schedule.
How does the job shadowing work?
As we grow our network of industry partners, high-performing StackUp students will be connected to professionals for structured shadowing experiences. This is actively being built and available to students who progress well through the program.
